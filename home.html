The Chemosynthetic Ocean World

Exploring life in an alien, space-like ocean

Energy Source

Organisms in this alien ocean world rely on chemosynthesis to survive, using chemicals like hydrogen sulfide (H2S) or methane (CH4) for energy.

Geological Features

The alien seafloor is rich in hydrothermal vents, volcanic activity, and mineral deposits. These features support life in the deep ocean.

Biosphere

The ecosystem is dominated by chemosynthetic microbes, but more complex life forms like tube worms may evolve.

Ecosystem Dynamics

Life thrives through symbiotic relationships. Chemosynthetic bacteria support larger organisms like tube worms, forming the basis of the ecosystem.
